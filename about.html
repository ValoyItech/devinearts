My Journey

The Story Behind Devine Arts

Devine Arts and Crafts is a proudly South African creative studio founded by Adam Thabane Malefofane, dedicated to producing unique, meaningful, and high-quality art. What started as a passion for customized footwear grew into a dynamic brand that now offers portraiture, wearable art, and creative craft solutions.

The company blends traditional artistic skill with modern customization techniques, creating art that people can both wear and treasure. From hand-painted shoes and apparel, to personalized portraits, every piece is infused with dedication, skill, and a commitment to excellence.

"Art is not what you see, but what you make others see. Every brushstroke is a conversation between the artist and the canvas."

With plans for screen printing and art classes on the horizon, Devine Arts and Crafts is driven by the vision of making art accessible, functional, and a part of everyday life. Today, we're proud to serve clients across South Africa and beyond, creating custom pieces that celebrate individuality and creativity.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To produce unique, meaningful, and high-quality art that blends traditional artistic skill with modern customization techniques. We're committed to creating art that people can both wear and treasure, delivering exceptional craftsmanship and unforgettable customer experiences.

To become South Africa's leading creative studio, making art accessible, functional, and a part of everyday life. Through wearable art, portraits, screen printing, and art classes, we envision a future where creativity is within everyone's reach and art becomes an integral part of daily living.
