About Us

Devine Arts and Crafts

Celebrating African Creativity Through Wearable and Visual Art

Our Story

Devine Arts and Crafts is a proudly South African creative studio founded by Adam Thabane Malefofane, dedicated to producing unique, meaningful, and high-quality art. What started as a passion for customized footwear grew into a dynamic brand that now offers portraiture, wearable art, and creative craft solutions.

The company blends traditional artistic skill with modern customization techniques, creating art that people can both wear and treasure. From hand-painted shoes and apparel, to personalized portraits, to plans for screen printing and art classes, Devine Arts and Crafts is driven by the vision of making art accessible, functional, and a part of everyday life.

Adam Thabane Malefofane

Founder & Lead Artist

"Art should be lived, worn, and experienced as part of everyday life."

Our Mission

To celebrate and promote African creativity and beauty through wearable and visual art, while empowering communities with artistic knowledge and opportunities.

Our Vision

To become a leading hub of creativity in South Africa, where art is not only admired but also lived, worn, and experienced.
